About Dubes Catering

Welcome to Dubes Catering, your premier choice for exquisite catering services in Gqeberha and the Eastern Cape. We specialize in creating unforgettable experiences for weddings, parties, and special events. With a passion for fresh, local ingredients and impeccable service, our team ensures every event is tailored to perfection.

Founded with love and expertise, Dubes Catering brings years of culinary passion to make your occasion truly special.

Our Services

  • Weddings: Customized menus for your big day, from elegant receptions to intimate ceremonies – plated dinners, buffets, or cocktail-style service.
  • Parties: Birthday celebrations, corporate events, or casual gatherings with delicious finger foods, braai options, and vibrant buffets.
  • Special Events: Anniversaries, baby showers, graduations, and corporate functions – we handle the details so you can enjoy the moment.

All packages include professional setup, elegant serving, and full cleanup. Contact us for a no-obligation personalized quote.
