About AllBusiness

AllBusiness is a modern, fast-growing platform that connects local customers with trusted businesses across Johannesburg and Gauteng.

We help small and medium businesses get discovered online — whether you're a restaurant, plumber, hair salon, gym, mechanic, tutor, or any other local service provider.

Our mission is simple: make it easy for people to find exactly what they need nearby, while giving business owners powerful, affordable tools to attract more customers.
