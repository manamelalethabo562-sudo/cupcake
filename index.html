My Story

Hello, I'm Lethabo, a Durban-based photographer passionate about capturing the raw beauty of everyday moments and the unique stories people carry.

My journey began with a simple camera and a desire to freeze time – from the vibrant streets of KwaZulu-Natal to quiet personal milestones. I believe every person, place, and moment has a story worth telling, and my goal is to tell it with honesty, warmth, and artistry.

Whether it's a wedding, portrait session, or commercial shoot, I focus on creating images that feel real, emotional, and timeless. Explore my themed galleries above to see my work organized by style and subject.

Let's create something beautiful together.
